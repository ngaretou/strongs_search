What is this?

When making an app with Scripture App Builder, builders have the option of defining their own menu items, and SAB provides an easy way of accomplishing this. Some language groups have multiple scripts and automatic transliteration processes like TECkit that can easily transliterate large amounts of text, and it can get tedious adding transliterated app menus one phrase at a time into SAB. This utility helps app builders by providing a way to quickly use their existing TECkit map or other transliteration process to quickly incorporate their automatically transliterated menus into SAB's menus.
